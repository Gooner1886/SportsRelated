Getting started with SportsRelated

Blog Feed

  • Blogs written by yourself and other users can be viewed on the Home page along with the SportsRelated sidebar that keeps users informed about any changes made to the Website

About Page

  • Clicking on the About link in the Navigation Bar will take users to SportsRelated's About page

My Blogs

  • My Blogs route helps users keep track of all their blogs and also allows them to update or delete them if and when required.

New Post

  • Users can post New Blogs by going to the New Post route
  • Uploading a new post will automatically bring the Blog to the feed for everyone to see

Account

  • Account Route shows users their account information

Log Out

  • Log Out allows users to log out of SportsRelated successfully.
